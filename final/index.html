Explore Philippines - Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Explore Philippines

The name "Explore Philippines" was selected because it clearly represents the mission of the website: to encourage visitors to discover and enjoy the natural beauty and attractions of the Philippines. It is simple, memorable, and highlights the adventurous spirit of travel. Since I am travelling to the Philippines soon I would like to create this site for my project.

Site Purpose

The website aims to promote local Philippine destinations, focusing on breathtaking beaches and stunning sceneries. It will offer travel guides, affordable vacation options, insider tips, and cultural highlights to inspire travelers to choose the Philippines for their next vacation without breaking their budget.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable beach resorts with excellent reviews?
  • What are the top attractions in the Philippines for leisure vacation?

Color Schema

Two primary colors have been selected for the site:

  • Deep Blue (#0077b6): Used for headings, navigation bars, and buttons to convey trust and tranquility.
  • Warm Orange (#f4a261): Used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and accents to add warmth and energy.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Roboto: Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to provide a modern and clean look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text and paragraphs to ensure readability and a friendly tone.
